The Siamese Cat

The Siamese cat is one of the first distinctly recognized breeds of Asian cat.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Common nickname: Meezer

Native to Thailand (formerly known as Siam), the Siamese became one of the most popular breeds in Europe and North America in the 19th century. The International Cat Association describes the modern Siamese as social, intelligent, and playful into adulthood, often enjoying a game of fetch. Siamese tend to seek human interaction and also like companionship from other cats.
